Privacy Policy

At Career Darshak, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you provide to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, and contact details.
  • Academic and career preferences shared during assessments.
  • Usage data such as browsing history, IP address, and cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized career guidance and assessments.
  • Improve our services and user experience.
  • Communicate updates, offers, and newsletters (with your consent).

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or to provide our services effectively.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.
  • Request the deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated to you through appropriate channels.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@careerdarshak.com.
